# Visits

The **Visits** workspace is where reception books appointments and doctors run
examinations. A visit carries everything about a single appointment: who the
patient is, which doctor and specialty, the examination record, and the charges.

## Booking a visit

Open the booking drawer from the Visits page and work top to bottom:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the specialty">
    The list of doctors filters to those who practice the chosen specialty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the doctor and subspecialty">
    Availability is checked against the doctor's weekly schedule for the date you
    pick. The subspecialty determines which examination template loads later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or create the patient">
    Search existing patients, or add a new one inline without leaving the drawer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a service and time">
    Choose the booked service (its price resolves from the default price list)
    and a date and time that fits the schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" title="Prerequisites">
You need a doctor with a specialty and an active schedule, plus a service catalog
and a default price list. See **[Getting started](/guide/getting-started)** if you
haven't set those up yet.
</Callout>

## Running the examination

Opening a visit loads its examination workspace. The form is driven by the
visit's subspecialty (falling back to the specialty), so each discipline sees the
fields that matter to it. During the visit the care team can:

- Record the examination and clinical notes.
- Add extra charges as services are delivered — each new charge starts as pending.
- Mark the visit completed when care is finished.

## From visit to invoice

Charges added during a visit stay pending until they're billed. Reception then
groups a visit's pending charges into an invoice and collects payment — covered
in **[Financial & billing](/guide/financial)**.
