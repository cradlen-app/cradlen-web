# Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is the home screen most of your team lands on after signing in
— a live overview of the clinic's activity this month that adapts to who's
looking.

## What you see

At the top: a welcome with your name, the organization and branch you're viewing,
and today's date. Below that, a set of cards and charts:

- **KPI cards** — attended visits, follow-ups, patients, and collected revenue,
  each with how it compares to last month.
- **Visit throughput** — a chart of attended visits and follow-ups day by day.
- **Financial overview** — invoiced, collected, outstanding, and your collection
  rate, with a daily trend and a link to the full reports.
- **Patient growth** — how your patient numbers are trending.
- **Staff** — a snapshot of your team.
- **Top doctors & top services** — this month's leaders by revenue.

All of it is **live** — pulled from your real visits, patients, and billing.

## It adapts to your role

<Callout type="note" title="You only see what's relevant to you">
**Owners and branch managers** see the full picture — revenue, staff, and top
performers included. **Doctors** see only **their own** activity (their visits,
follow-ups, and patients), without the financial, staff, or top-performer cards.
**Reception and accountants** don't land here at all — they open straight into
**Visits** and **Financial** respectively. See
**[Roles & permissions](/guide/roles-and-permissions)**.
</Callout>

## One branch or all of them

If you're an owner with more than one branch, a scope switch at the top lets you
flip the whole dashboard between **this branch** and **all branches** (an
organization-wide roll-up).

For the detailed numbers behind the financial cards, open
**[Financial & billing](/guide/financial)**.
