# Settings & subscription

**Settings** is where you manage your own profile and — if you're the owner — the
organization, its branches, and the subscription. What you see depends on your
role.

## The settings sections

- **Profile** — your personal details (name, phone, specialty).
- **Account** — your password and security.
- **Organization** *(owner)* — the organization's name, details, and logo.
- **Branches** *(owner)* — add, edit, or remove branches. Branches share patient
  records but each has its own staff, schedule, and visits.
- **Subscription** *(owner)* — your plan, add-ons, and billing.
- **Danger zone** *(owner)* — delete a branch or the organization.

## Your subscription

The Subscription section shows your **current plan**, its **status** (trial,
active, expired, or cancelled), the renewal or trial-end date, and your usage
against the plan's **limits** (branches and staff). Four plans are available:

- **Free Trial** — 1 branch, 5 staff, 14 days.
- **Individual Clinic** — 1 branch, 2 staff.
- **Center** — 1 branch, 10 staff.
- **Network of Centers** — 3 branches, 25 staff.

Pick a plan card to **upgrade** (or **renew** if it has expired).

## Add-ons

Add-ons give you extra capacity **on top of your plan** without moving to a
larger tier. There are two:

- **Extra branch** — adds a branch (and the user seats that come with it).
- **Extra user** — adds staff seats.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Make sure your plan is active">
    Add-ons need an **active paid plan** — you can't buy them on a trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the one you need">
    Click **Add** on the add-on. For extra users, choose how many seats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how to pay">
    Pick a payment method (InstaPay or mobile wallet) and continue to payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" title="Pro-rata and effective limits">
Add-ons are **charged pro-rata** to the time left in your term, so you only pay
for the remaining period. Once verified, your add-ons appear as badges and raise
your effective limits — the branches and staff you're allowed.
</Callout>

## Billing & payments

The **Payments** page lists every subscription payment with its purpose (a
**plan** or an **add-on**) and its status — pending, awaiting verification,
verified, rejected, or cancelled. For a manual payment, open it to see the
**pay-to details, amount, and reference**, complete the transfer, then upload
your receipt; the status moves to *awaiting verification* and then *verified*.
You can cancel a payment that's still pending.

## Who can manage it

<Callout type="note" title="Subscription and org settings are owner-only">
Everyone can edit their own **Profile** and **Account**. Only **owners** can
manage the **Organization**, **Branches**, the **Subscription** (including
add-ons), and the **Danger zone**. Branch managers, doctors, reception, and
accountants can't change org settings or the plan. See
**[Roles & permissions](/guide/roles-and-permissions)**.
</Callout>
